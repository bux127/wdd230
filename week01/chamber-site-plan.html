Ezulwini Chamber of Commerce

This is a town name which the chamber operates

Purpose

Ezulwini Camber of Commerce serves more than one purpose, it presents and promotes tourists attraction hotspots, it promotes local business and orgnizations and brings together local business people with same interests.

It will also present a scope of cultural dynamics and hand-made products on sale, this functions to support diverse entrepreneurship approaches and fostering workforce development.

Scenarios

  • How is the town unique from other existing towns?
  • What status quo cultural events will the chamber be holding?
  • Which tourists attraction are rated best?
  • What are the benefits for joining the chamber?
